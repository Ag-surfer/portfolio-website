## About Section Structure

### The HERO Framework
- **H**ook: One compelling sentence
- **E**xperience: Your journey
- **R**eason: Why you do this
- **O**utside: Personal touch

### Template
```markdown
## About Me

[Hook - 1 sentence that captures attention]

### My Journey
[2-3 sentences about your background and how you got here]

### How I Work
[Your approach, philosophy, or what makes you different]

### Beyond Code
[Personal interests, hobbies, what makes you human]

### Currently
[What you're working on, learning, or excited about]
```

### Example
```markdown
## About Me

I believe the best digital experiences are invisible â€” they just work.

### My Journey
I started building websites in high school, teaching myself HTML in 
the school library. That curiosity led me through a CS degree at 
Stanford and five years crafting products at companies like Airbnb 
and Notion.

### How I Work
I obsess over the details. Every interaction, every animation, every 
error message is an opportunity to make someone's day a little better. 
I combine technical depth with design sensibility to build products 
that are both beautiful and bulletproof.

### Beyond Code
When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me hiking the Bay Area 
trails, attempting (and failing) at watercolor painting, or hunting 
for the perfect bowl of ramen.

### Currently
I'm exploring the intersection of AI and design systems, building 
tools that help designers work smarter.
```

## Project Descriptions

### The STAR Format
- **S**ituation: Context and challenge
- **T**ask: Your specific role
- **A**ction: What you did
- **R**esult: Measurable outcome

### Template
```markdown
## [Project Name]

### Overview
[1-2 sentences describing the project]

### The Challenge
[What problem needed solving? Who was it for?]

### My Role
[What did you specifically do?]

### The Approach
[How did you solve it? Key decisions made]

### Key Features
- [Feature 1]: [Brief benefit]
- [Feature 2]: [Brief benefit]
- [Feature 3]: [Brief benefit]

### Results
[Metrics, outcomes, or impact]

### Tech Stack
[Technologies used]

### Links
[Live Site] [GitHub] [Case Study]
```

### Example
```markdown

complex state management at scale.

### The Challenge

### Key Features
- **Block-based editing**: Nested pages, toggles, callouts, code blocks
- **Real-time collaboration**: See others' cursors and changes instantly

- **Markdown support**: Write naturally, format automatically
```

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Do
- Be conversational but professional
- Use "I" and "you" â€” it's personal
- Show confidence without arrogance
- Be specific with numbers and details
- Write in active voice
- Keep sentences short

### Don't
- Use jargon or buzzwords
- Be vague ("various projects")
- Sound corporate or stiff
- Over-promise or exaggerate
- Use passive voice
- Write walls of text

### Word Swaps
| Instead of | Use |
|------------|-----|

| Synergy | Teamwork |
| Innovative | New, fresh |
| Solutions | Products, tools |
| Experienced professional | I've worked on |
| Passionate about | I love, I enjoy |
